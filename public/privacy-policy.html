Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

HOW COME? does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

About This App

HOW COME? is an educational app for Kerala PSC English Grammar preparation. It provides bilingual (English + Malayalam) explanations for past PSC question papers to help students prepare for government exams.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Account credentials (there are no accounts)
  • Payment information (payments are handled securely by Google Play)

How the App Works

HOW COME? is a static web application. All content — questions, answer options, and Malayalam explanations — is stored directly in the app and loaded locally on your device. No data is sent to any server when you use the app.

The app works offline after the first visit, using a service worker to cache content on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app is hosted on Vercel (vercel.com), which may collect standard server logs (such as IP address and browser type) as part of normal hosting operations. This is governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.

We do not use any advertising networks, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services.

Children's Privacy

HOW COME? is suitable for users of all ages. Since we collect no personal information, there is no risk to children's privacy. The app complies with applicable children's privacy laws.

Data Security

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to secure or protect on our end. All app content is publicly available educational material.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
