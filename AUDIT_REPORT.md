## STANDARD THAT MUST BE MET

Every explanation must follow the pattern from 54-2026-M (our gold standard):

**correct section:** State answer + explain WHY in Malayalam. Every technical term must have its Malayalam meaning in brackets. Eg: "Passive voice (കർമ്മണി പ്രയോഗം) ആണ്..."

**rule section:** Grammar rule stated with Malayalam + 3 examples, each with Malayalam translation. Eg: "1. She is making tea. → Tea is being made. (ചായ ഉണ്ടാക്കപ്പെടുന്നു)"

**wrong section:** Each wrong option explained in Malayalam, not just labelled wrong. Eg: "'Was made' Simple Past ആണ്, Present Continuous അല്ല — tense തെറ്റ്."

**tip section:** Memory trick entirely in Malayalam conversational style. PSC shortcut tips in Malayalam. Eg: "PSC-ൽ ഈ trap ആവർത്തിക്കുന്നു! ആദ്യം subject നോക്കുക, verb match ചെയ്യുക."
